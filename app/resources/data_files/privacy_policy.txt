### Privacy Policy for Tracks

**Last Updated:** October 28, 2024

Welcome to *Tracks*! This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application, *Tracks*. Please read this privacy policy carefully to understand our views and practices regarding your personal data and how we will treat it.

### 1. Information We Collect

#### a. Personal Information
We may collect the following personal information when you register for and use *Tracks*:
- **Profile Information**: such as username, email address, and profile picture.
- **Communication Data**: including text messages, images, and audio files that you send or receive.

#### b. Usage Data
We collect usage data, including but not limited to:
- **Activity Information**: interactions with other users, messages sent and received, group participation, and audio and music playback activity.
- **Device Information**: type of mobile device, IP address, operating system, and other unique identifiers.
---
#### c. Firebase Data
We use Firebase for user authentication, database storage, and real-time communication. Firebase may collect certain types of data for functionality and performance improvements, including crash data and usage metrics.

#### d. Advertising Data
Our app uses AdMob to serve personalized and non-personalized ads. AdMob may collect information, including device information, app usage data, and location data, to deliver tailored advertisements.

### 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide, maintain, and improve *Tracks* and its features.
- Facilitate messaging and media-sharing functionalities.
- Allow users to connect and listen to music simultaneously.
- Monitor and analyze usage and trends to improve user experience.
- Personalize advertising through AdMob based on user behaviour and preferences.

### 3. How We Share Your Information

#### a. With Third-Party Service Providers
We may share your information with service providers like Firebase and AdMob to facilitate app functionality and support personalized advertising.

#### b. Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court or a government agency).

### 4. Data Security

We take appropriate security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access and disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

### 5. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal data. These rights may include the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict our use of your data. To exercise these rights, please contact us at [Contact Information].

### 6. Children's Privacy

*Tracks* is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected data from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information.

### 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page. You are advised to review this policy periodically for any updates.
